Sales Collateral Library

Pick a brand to open its one-pager, then hit Download PDF (choose “Save as PDF”). Every sheet pulls the live network map, branch list, and counts — so collateral is always current, no design tools required.

Brand logos and copy live in assets/js/collateral-brands.js — edit there and every one-pager updates. Brands without a logo file render a clean wordmark until one is added to assets/collateral/logos/.
